Lasagna

Recipe Description

This Lasagna Recipe is easy to make with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. BONUS: itâ€™s make-ahead and freezer-friendly!

This is a great freezer meal, make-ahead recipe, and leftovers freeze and reheat super well.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta cheese mixture
  • Meat sauce
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased casserole dish
  2. Add 4 boiled lasagna noodles (or use no-boil or fresh noodles)
  3. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture on top. Add 1.5 cups of meat sauce
  4. Repeat the same layerstwo more times
