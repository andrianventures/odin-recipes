Pizza

Recipe Description

This homemade pizza recipe is perfect for parties! Everyone loves pizza, and your guests can customize their toppings to suit their personal tastes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dough
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Make the dough into a flat pie
  2. Add cheese and pepperoni
  3. Cook the pie in 450 degree oven for 10 minutes
